Dear Parents,

Seems like you and your child just made a pledge to place family time before media usage time.


Kid’s Pledge

  • I will never give out personal information online or by text.
  • I understand my parents have the right to check my media history.
  • I will try to keep my total screen time to 2 hours a day.
  • I will not watch shows or play games that are inappropriate for me.

Parents’ Pledge

  • I will check what my kids are doing online and on their phones and let them know before I check their digital devices.
  • I will talk to my kids about the digital world and show interest in what they do online.
  • I will help them make good media choices.
  • If my child makes a mistake, I will ask questions and learn what happened.

Parents and Kids’ Pledge

  • We will talk as a family at a meal a day with no technology use!
  • We will agree to technology-free times such as meals, weekends, and vacations.
  • We won’t sacrifice important family time for media or digital use.
  • We agree to use technology responsibly.

Thanks for helping your iZ HERO out! For more information check what programmes are available for you and your child on iZHEROAlliance.org.
